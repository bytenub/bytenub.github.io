Born on the racetrack.
Built for the road.

The new Audi R8 Coupe new standards in performance with 610 horsepower, 330 km/h and acceleration from 0 to 100 in just 3.2 seconds. The Audi R8 V10 plus combines breathtaking performance with aesthetics, to create one of the most striking and powerful Audi production cars ever built.

Pushing the performance

The even more powerful V10 naturally aspirated engine leaves a lasting impression. Developed according to the high-speed concept, it fascinates with its fast response and enormous power over the entire speed range. Available in Audi R8 V10 plus with 449 kW or the Audi R8 V10 with 397 kW.

Fall in love faster

Experience maximum racing character in connection with outstanding design: the striking single-frame Audi, the muscular sidelines and the athletic rear underline the exceptional design. The focus is on the V10 mid-engine, which is below the rear window and can also be lit on request.

Audi Matrix Laser Headlights

The optional laser Highbeam doubles the range of the LED headlights on 600 meters. A laser module is integrated per headlight, in which four high-performance laser diodes are used. A phosphor converter converts the blue laser beam in a traffic-grade white light for added visibility and safety

Audi Virtual Cockpit

Concentrating on the essentials: The instrument cluster in the form of a high-resolution 12.3-inch LCD screens informs the driver in fascinating graphics. You can switch between different display modes and adjust the instrument cluster to your own needs, making for an absolutely unforgettable view.
